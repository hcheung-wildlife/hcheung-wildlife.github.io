About Me

Welcome to my page! My is Nicole Lee and I currently reside in Hong Kong. I grew up in Hong Kong and then went to Canada and USA for high school and college, respectively. I graduated from college not too long ago with a B.S. in Corporate Finance & Accounting and I am currently in a Business Development role for a product design company.

I started the software developer track in February 2017 and I am looking forward to all the work I will be doing. When I’m not working on Bloc or at work, I’m playing competitive dodgeball. That’s right, dodgeball.

Feel free to look around my page and take a look at my different projects or photos that I take. If you want to know more about me, my work, or you just want to chat about music, food, and travel, drop me a note at the bottom of the page!
